A Little Bit About Me

_Date: August 23, 2023 | Author: Youkai Princess

I'm Artist

Yeah, I love making art!

Drawing, modeling, photography, dancing, poetry... I love and try to reproduce every form of art that I enjoy.

I also make DIY crafts, from small repurposed devices to desks

My PC is set up on a desk I made. I like to see the RGB Leds through the acrylic top.

A Shy Beauty

I'm lowprofile. I don't like much to be seen.

Think in me as a modern-days kunoichi. I do my quests for the sake of doing, not to exihibit myself.

But I'm a pretty trans girl

A cute androginous face. Slender build, fitness body and a style that fits.

Again, like a urban kunoichi. Maybe a Goth Sorceress Kunoichi, but anyway...

More About Visual Expression

Yeah, I'm shy.

But I like to do cosplay.

I have no courage to post anywhere.

But who see me, they always get surprised.

This is something I'm working on now!

Open Souce Enthusiast

One thing I love is to contribute on Open Source Projects.

I use mostly Open Source tools for work. Sometimes I feel an urge to help who help me.

For me it's just a natural feeling.

But it makes me give a hand everytime I see a chance.

The problem is when the lack of self confidence hits me. But it's another thing I'm improving now!
